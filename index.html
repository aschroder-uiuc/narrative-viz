AKC Breed Ranking of 2016

Large Breed Dominance

The American Kennel Club releases yearly rankings of the popularity of each recognized breed, quantified by the number of registrations of the breed for that year.

The most popular breeds are generally "family-friendly" large-breed dogs - energetic and friendly, good with kids and families, but often needing large amounts of space and exercise.

Referred to as "America's Dog", the Labrador Retriever has been the record-holder for the most popular breed, grabbing Number One position in the breed ranking since 1991.
