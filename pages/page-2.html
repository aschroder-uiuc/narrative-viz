The Rise of the French Bulldog

AKC Breed Ranking of 2020

The French Bulldog's Meteoric Rise

First making it into the Top 5 in 2017, it takes only 3 years for the French Bulldog to start nipping at the Labrador Retriever's heels in the number 2 position.

The smallest dogs in the Top 5, Frenchies are jam-packed full of playful, goofy personality. Their small size makes them fantastic city dogs, and their outgoing nature means that they are usually great with kids and other dogs alike.

Rounding out the top 5, the Golden Retriever, German Shepherd Dog, and English Bulldog maintain their popularity.
