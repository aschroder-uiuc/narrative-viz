The Rise of the French Bulldog

AKC Breed Ranking of 2022

Frenchie Dominance

Beating out the Labrador Retriever, the heavyweight champ the AKC rankings, the French Bulldog becomes the most popular dog breed in America in 2022!

This is the Frenchie's first time in the top spot, with registrations increasing by over 1,000% since 2012, when it was ranked 14th. Small, friendly, and adaptable, it's no surprise that the popularity of the French Bulldog rose in conjunction with increased adoptions during COVID.

Will it continue to reign supreme? Check back next year when the AKC releases the 2023 rankings!
